Zen Dental

Browse Prototype By Device Type

Mobile
Desktop

Brief

Zen Dental is a fully functional dental clinic website designed with the primary goal of generating leads and simplifying the process of making an appointment. The design ensures users can book an appointment effortlessly, without needing to search very hard for this section

Key Features

  • Seamless Appointment Booking: A user-friendly appointment creation page
  • Innovative Design Elements: Inverted border-radius sections for a modern aesthetic
  • Engaging Video Content: Videos featuring doctors explaining the processes behind their offered services

Process

The focus of the design was to align with Zen Dental's mission—making dental visits a peaceful experience. Just as the clinic aims to reduce stress for patients, the website was designed to provide a hassle-free navigation experience that feels calm, intuitive, and welcoming
